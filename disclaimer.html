Usage Notice

GeoSphere uses third-party content to help you explore places in context.

GeoSphere combines map, news, and trivia services to make place discovery faster and more engaging. The information shown is useful for exploration and learning, but it should not be treated as official or guaranteed accurate.

Last updated: April 10, 2026 Third-party live content

1. Content Sources

Maps, headlines, and trivia come from third-party services.

GeoSphere depends on external providers such as Mapbox, WorldNewsAPI, OpenTriviaDB, and Numbers API. Their availability, pricing, policies, and response quality can change at any time without notice.

2. Accuracy

Information may be incomplete, delayed, or approximate.

News results may reflect partial matches, syndicated reporting, or API limits. Trivia is intended for learning and fun, not for academic verification. Geographic labels and coordinates should not be relied on for safety-critical decisions.

3. Local Storage

Your favorites and recent searches are stored in this browser.

GeoSphere uses local browser storage to remember favorites, session state, and search history. That data stays on the device unless the browser is cleared or the user manually removes it.

4. External Links

News cards can point to sites outside GeoSphere.

When you open an article, you leave this project and enter a third-party site with its own privacy, security, and content policies. Review those sources directly if you need authoritative information.

5. Important Note

Do not use GeoSphere as your only source for critical decisions.

GeoSphere is suitable for exploration, discovery, and lightweight learning, but it is not legal, financial, medical, emergency, or navigation advice.
